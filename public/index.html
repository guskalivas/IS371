Have you ever gone on Instagram, Snapchat or any other social media and seen a song posted - you listen to it
and like it, but when you go back the next day its gone? This website is the solution!

All you have to do is:

1) Find a song you enjoy listening to and want to share with your friends

2) Click the "Make a Post" button, fill in all the song details, and post the song

3) Scroll through your friends post to see more songs to add to your playlists
